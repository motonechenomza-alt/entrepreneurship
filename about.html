Our Startup Story

From a Dorm Room Idea to a Movement

In 2024, our founder Nomza Motoneche was a first year university student who loved coffee but hated the environment impact. Every day, she saw thousands of single-use coffee cups being thrown away. That's when the ispiration stuck

With just M5000.00 in savings and a mission to make coffee sustainable, EcoBrew was born. What started as a small online store selling compostable coffee pods has now grown into a movement of over 10,000 concisious coffee drinkers.

Today we are proud to say ever EcoBrew purchase helps:

  • 🌎 plant tree in coffee-growing regions
  • 🤝 support fair wages for 500+ farming families
  • ♻️ diver 10,000+ platic cups from landfills
